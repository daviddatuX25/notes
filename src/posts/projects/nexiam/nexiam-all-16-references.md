## 1. Unified Admission Systems

### 01 — Adwan et al. (2023) ✓

**Citation**: Adwan, E. J., Al-Aradi, Y., Essa, M., & Malabari, H. (2023). A comprehensive study and development of unified mobile-based admission system for GCC universities. *Journal of Computing Theories and Applications*, *1*(2), 212–230. https://doi.org/10.62411/jcta.9793

**Abstract**: The Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) region has experienced tremendous growth in the higher education sector... This research project aims to provide a unified management tool to manage the admission and tracking process to multiple accredited GCC universities through one unified central mobile-based application. An Agile-SDLC methodology was employed... the empirical findings revealed 97.7% of usability.

## 2. AI/ML in College Admissions

### 05 — Lira et al. (2023) ✓
*Year corrected from 2025 → **2023** (Science Advances)*

**Citation**: Lira, B., Gardner, M., Quirk, A., Stone, C., Rao, A., Ungar, L., Hutt, S., Hickman, L., D'Mello, S. K., & Duckworth, A. L. (2023). Using artificial intelligence to assess personal qualities in college admissions. *Science Advances*, *9*(41), eadg9405. https://doi.org/10.1126/sciadv.adg9405

**Key findings**: ML models assess personal qualities from application essays with adequate accuracy; assessments correlate with life outcomes years later; fine-tuning can mitigate performance disparities. Published in *Science Advances* — high-impact, peer-reviewed.

---

### 06 — Van Busum & Fang (2025) ✓
*Year corrected from 2024 → **2025** (Neural Computing and Applications)*

**Relevance**: Directly supports NEXIAM's human-in-the-loop K-Means design — AI admission models must address bias, which counselor oversight mitigates.

## How to Funnel into the Background Section

**Current gap**: Manuscript mentions global exam problems → PH policy mandates → ISPSC field findings → NEXIAM.

**Strengthened funnel**: What exists globally (Adwan, Lira, Van Busum) → what exists in PH HEIs (Dayata, Catalan, Cuerdo) → what exists at ISPSC (Yukee, Ballesteros) → none integrate into a single governed pipeline under RA 10173 → NEXIAM fills this gap.

**Three strongest new precedents to cite**:
1. **Dayata & Yap** — RAG for PH university policies (direct chatbot precedent)
2. **Cuerdo et al.** — OMR in PH public schools (direct OMR precedent)
3. **Tan** — K-Means for course selection (direct clustering precedent)
