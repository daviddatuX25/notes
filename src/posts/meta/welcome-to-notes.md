This is a personal notes site. It's built with [Eleventy](https://www.11ty.dev/)
and designed to be a clean reading app — no sidebars, no comments, no noise.

## What goes here

AI-generated content. Essays, summaries, write-ups. Things I want to
re-read later or share with someone.

## Keyboard shortcuts

Press `?` to see all shortcuts. The main ones:

- `j` / `k` — next / previous post
- `g g` — back to index
- `t` — toggle theme (light / dark / system)

## Internal links

Links between notes work like a wiki. If I reference another note, I just
link to it: [welcome post](./welcome-to-notes).

## Dark mode

The site supports light, dark, and system themes. Press `t` to cycle. Your
choice is remembered. In system mode, it follows your OS setting and updates
live when you change it.

That's it. No comments, no analytics, no cookie banner. Just text.
